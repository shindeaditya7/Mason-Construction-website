    Below are answers to some of the most common questions about our services, processes, and project timelines. Still need help? Contact us directly and one of our estimators will get back to you within 24 hours.

    • What types of projects does Mason Construction handle?

      We manage commercial and residential projects including facade restorations under Local Laws 11 and 126, roof replacements, interior fit-outs with custom millwork, and concrete site work. Our team oversees from estimate to finish, ensuring quality and compliance. and rigorous safety. Always Trusted by New York!

    • How do I request a free estimate and what’s included?

      To request a free estimate, fill contact form or call (347) 933-0867 with project details (address, scope, photos, desired start date). Always Trusted by New York!

    • What is your typical project timeline?

      Project timelines vary based on scope and code requirements. West Village façade restoration took twelve months; a $1.8M 23 Cornelia St renovation took nine months; a $1.5M 18 Harrison St interior fit-out lasted six months. Smaller roof or tuckpointing jobs typically finish in four weeks. Always Trusted by New York!

    • Do you offer warranties or post-job support?

      Every Mason Construction project comes with a one-year workmanship warranty and applicable warranty on materials. We conduct a final walkthrough to address punch-list items within five business days. After completion, our office remains available for maintenance inquiries or scheduling inspections. Always Trusted by New York!
